🎨 Kids Photo Printing Studio

About the Project

Welcome to the ultimate creative space for kids! This studio allows children to drag, drop, and arrange their favorite photos for school projects or fun collages without the complexity of traditional editors.

  • Marquee Select: Click and drag empty space to select multiple photos.
  • Pro Tools Built-in: Use Ctrl+Drag to clone patterns, and drag handles inward on edges to crop!
  • Infinite Undo: Mistakes are instantly erased with the Undo button or Ctrl+Z.
